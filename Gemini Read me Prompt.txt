Goal/Problem: My machine learning projects predicts the price of ticket for several different airlines. It solves the problem of predicting the price of the airlines from several different sources and destinations, for a time duration and total stops.

Data Source: Sourced from EaseMyTrip.com

Note:- Airline (count): 11, Source (count): 5 and Destination (count): 6. I have used One hot encoder with drop='first', excluded airline: Air Asia and excluded Source as well as Destination: Bangalore.

Models used: Linear Regression, Lasso, Decision Tree Regressor, Extra Trees Regressor, Random Forest Regressor, Ada Boost Regressor, LightGBM Regressor, XGBoost Regressor, KNeighbors Regressor and Support Vector Regressor. Also, tuned those models with GridSearchCV and RandomizedSearchCV.
Hyperparameter tuned LightGBM Regressor was the final model as it had the best score.

Evaluation Metrics: I used R2_score, MAE, MSE, RMSE and MAPE as evaluation metrics.
